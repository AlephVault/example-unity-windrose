Detailed Description

Vision ranges are related to Watcher objects. They have a way to tell their own dimensions but are strictly tied to such watcher objects, who receive all the events. Think of the regions in Pokemon games where you step and a trainer spots you and a fight starts.

Usually, this object is related to watchers, and nothing needs to be done here. However, this component can be created on its own, provided the relatedPositionable is filled accordingly.

Vision ranges spread to certain direction (being specified or being taken from the related positionable's Oriented component), with a given length (considering a base of 1), and a given width (considering a base of 1, and spreading to each side of the main, oriented, spread).
