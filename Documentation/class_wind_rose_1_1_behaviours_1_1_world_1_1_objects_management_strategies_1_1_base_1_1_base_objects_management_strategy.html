Detailed Description

Base management strategies will only check object movement by ensuring their dimensions are never out of the map's bounds.

Checking whether movement can be cleared involves checking whether movement is being performed, and its counterpart is Objects.Strategies.Base.BaseObjectStrategy.

See also
ObjectsManagementStrategy, Objects.Strategies.Base.BaseObjectStrategy

uint  y 
)
virtual

This method may be implemented to tell how will the strategy compute the data for a single cell. This will happen at initialization and at cell update. The default implementation does nothing.

Parameters
xThe X position of the cell(s) being taken for computation
yThe Y position of the cell(s) being taken for computation

One should take into account the ObjectsManagementStrategyHolder.Tilemaps property to iterate them to know the cells and fetch their data most likely by invoking ScriptableObjects.Tiles.BundledTile.GetStrategyFrom<T>(UnityEngine.Tilemaps.TileBase) to get their associated data.
