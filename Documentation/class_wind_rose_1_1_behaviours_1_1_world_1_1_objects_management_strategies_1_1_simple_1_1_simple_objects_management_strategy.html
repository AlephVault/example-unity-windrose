Detailed Description

Combines the power of Base.LayoutObjectsManagementStrategy which forbids walking through blocked cells, and Solidness.SolidnessObjectsManagementStrategy which forbids solid objects walking through occupied cells.

Its counterpart is Objects.Strategies.Simple.SimpleObjectStrategy.
