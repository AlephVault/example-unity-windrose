Detailed Description

Solidness masks are an bidimensional mask of integers telling to what extent is the map occupied. Dimensions of this mask will match the dimensions of the map needing it, and each cell's values involves to what degree the cell is overlapped by solid or solid-for-others (or hole) objects.
