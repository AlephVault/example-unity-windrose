◆ PagingType

enum GabTab.Behaviours.Interactors.PagingType
strong

Paging type implies the pagination technique used by the ListIterator. We can illustrate the differences by considering the following example:

You have 13 elements, 3 displays, and you are after 3 moves to NEXT page (displaying items 9 10 and 11), and you go NEXT one more time.

  • Snapped You will get items[12] nothing nothing rendered, and you can't move further.
  • Looping You will get items[12] items[0] items[1] rendered, and you can still move further: next page being rendered will involve items 2, 3, and 4.
  • Clamped You will get items[10] items[11] items[12] rendered, and you can't move further. If you move back from there, you will render elements 7, 8, and 9 instead of the original 9, 10, and 11. After two more back movements you will render elements 1, 2, and 3. One more back will leave you at elements 0, 1, and 2.
